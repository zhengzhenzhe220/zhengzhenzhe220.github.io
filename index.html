About me

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a Post-Doc in the Coordinated Science Laboratory (CSL) of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. R. Srikant. I received my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao University in March, 2018 and was advised by Prof. Fan Wu and Prof. Guihai Chen. My research interests are in the intersection of economics and networking. In particular, I have focused on game theory, machine learning and their applications for network economics, computer networking, and mobile computing.

Research Interests

  • Game Theory

  • Network Economics

  • Wireless Network and Mobile Computing

  • Machine Learning
